# Phase 01: Discovery and Problem Definition

## Objective
Define who the product serves, what problems matter most, and how success is measured.

## Core Activities
- Identify target personas for LMS/ERP context:
  - Students
  - Teachers
  - School Admin
  - Municipality/Management
  - Parents
- Define core pain points and measurable business impact.
- Perform competitive analysis:
  - Feature gaps
  - Pricing benchmarks
  - Positioning opportunities
- Build Product Requirement Document (PRD).

## Inputs
- Interviews with pilot schools and internal stakeholders.
- Existing discovery docs in `.agent/product-discovery/`.
- Market and pricing data.

## Deliverables
- PRD (problem statement, scope boundaries, non-goals).
- Persona set with top jobs-to-be-done.
- Success KPI sheet:
  - Activation rate
  - Monthly active users by role
  - Retention
  - Feature adoption
  - Support ticket volume

## Exit Criteria
- Top 3 problem statements signed by product + engineering.
- KPI baseline and targets documented.
- PRD approved for roadmap scoping.

## File Outputs
- `templates/prd-template.md` (create project-specific copy)
- `templates/persona-template.md` (create project-specific copy)
