# Phase 10: Beta Launch (Pilot Schools)

## Objective
Validate product-market fit, usability, and operational readiness with real institutions.

## Core Activities
- Onboard 1-5 pilot schools.
- Gather feedback from admins, teachers, and students.
- Track adoption and reliability metrics.
- Fix high-impact usability and stability gaps quickly.
- Improve onboarding and training material.

## Inputs
- Hardened staging/production candidate build.

## Deliverables
- Pilot school feedback report.
- Beta KPI dashboard:
  - Activation
  - Retention
  - Performance
  - Support issue volume
- Updated onboarding assets.

## Exit Criteria
- Pilot KPIs meet agreed thresholds.
- Critical usability blockers resolved.
- Product and support teams approve production launch.

## File Outputs
- `templates/beta-launch-template.md`
