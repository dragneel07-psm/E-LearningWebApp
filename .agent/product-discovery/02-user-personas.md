## Persona 1: School Administrator (Principal/Admin)

### Demographics
- **Name**: Rajesh Kumar
- **Age**: 45
- **Role**: Principal
- **School Size**: 300 students
- **Location**: Kathmandu, Nepal
- **Tech Savviness**: Medium

### Background
Rajesh has been a principal for 8 years at a growing private school. He manages a team of 25 teachers and handles both academic and administrative responsibilities. He's frustrated with juggling multiple spreadsheets and manual processes.

### Goals & Motivations
- **Primary Goal**: Improve school efficiency and reduce administrative burden
- **Secondary Goals**:
  - Increase student enrollment through better reputation
  - Make data-driven decisions about school operations
  - Ensure financial transparency and accountability
  - Improve parent satisfaction and communication

### Pain Points
- Spends 10+ hours/week on manual data entry and report generation
- Difficulty tracking student performance across classes
- Challenges in fee collection and financial reporting
- Limited visibility into teacher effectiveness
- Parent complaints about lack of communication

### Behaviors & Preferences
- Checks email 3-4 times daily
- Prefers dashboard views with key metrics
- Needs mobile access for on-the-go management
- Values detailed reports for board meetings
- Wants automated notifications for critical events

### User Stories
1. "As a principal, I want to see real-time enrollment and attendance data so I can identify trends and issues quickly"
2. "As an admin, I need to generate financial reports with one click so I can present to the board without manual work"
3. "As a school leader, I want to track teacher performance metrics so I can provide targeted support and recognition"
4. "As an administrator, I need to send school-wide announcements so parents stay informed about important events"

### Success Criteria
- Reduces administrative time by 30%
- Generates monthly reports in <5 minutes
- 90% parent engagement with platform
- Clear visibility into all school operations

## Persona 2: Teacher

- **Age**: 32

- **Teaching Experience**: 7 years
- **Class Size**: 30-35 students per class
- **Location**: Delhi, India
- **Tech Savviness**: Medium-High

- Difficult to provide detailed feedback to all students
- Attendance tracking is time-consuming
- Hard to identify which students need extra help
- Parent meetings are reactive, not proactive
- Limited insights into individual learning patterns

### Behaviors & Preferences
- Uses laptop for lesson planning, phone for quick tasks
- Prefers simple, intuitive interfaces
- Needs quick access to student information
- Values automation for repetitive tasks
- Wants to focus on teaching, not technology

### User Stories
1. "As a teacher, I want AI to grade objective assessments automatically so I can focus on providing qualitative feedback"
2. "As an educator, I need to see which students are struggling with specific topics so I can provide targeted support"
3. "As a teacher, I want to mark attendance with one click so I don't waste class time on roll calls"
4. "As an instructor, I need to share lesson materials easily so students can access them anytime"
5. "As a teacher, I want to communicate with parents about student progress so we can work together to help the student"

### Success Criteria
- Reduces grading time by 50%

## Persona 3: Student

### Demographics
- **Name**: Aarav Patel
- **Age**: 15
- **Grade**: 10th
- **Location**: Mumbai, India
- **Tech Savviness**: High
- **Device Access**: Smartphone, shared laptop

### Background
Aarav is a motivated student preparing for board exams. He struggles with Physics and needs extra help but is too shy to ask questions in class. He uses his phone for most online activities and prefers learning at his own pace.

### Goals & Motivations
- **Primary Goal**: Improve grades and understand difficult concepts
- **Secondary Goals**:
  - Get help with homework without feeling embarrassed
  - Track own progress and identify weak areas
  - Access study materials anytime, anywhere
  - Prepare effectively for exams
  - Stay organized with assignments and deadlines

- Afraid to ask questions in class
- Doesn't know where to get help after school hours
- Forgets assignment deadlines
- Unsure about exam preparation strategy
- Difficult to find quality study resources
- Can't track own progress effectively

### Behaviors & Preferences
- Primarily uses smartphone (80% of time)
- Prefers video explanations over text
- Studies late evening/night
- Likes gamification and progress tracking
- Shares resources with friends
- Active on social media

### User Stories
1. "As a student, I want to ask the AI tutor questions anytime so I can get help when I'm stuck on homework"
2. "As a learner, I need to see my progress on each subject so I know where to focus my study time"
3. "As a student, I want to access all my course materials in one place so I don't have to search multiple platforms"
4. "As a learner, I need reminders for upcoming assignments so I never miss a deadline"
5. "As a student, I want personalized practice questions so I can improve in my weak areas"

### Success Criteria
- Uses AI tutor 3+ times per week
- Completes 90% of assignments on time
- Improves grades by one level in weak subjects
- Feels confident asking for help

## Persona 4: Parent

### Demographics
- **Name**: Sunita Gupta
- **Age**: 42
- **Role**: Working Mother
- **Children**: 2 (ages 12 and 15)
- **Occupation**: Marketing Manager
- **Location**: Bangalore, India
- **Tech Savviness**: Medium

### Background
Sunita is a busy professional who wants to be involved in her children's education but struggles to find time. She attends parent-teacher meetings when possible but wishes she had more regular updates about her children's progress.

### Goals & Motivations
- **Primary Goal**: Stay informed about children's academic progress
- **Secondary Goals**:
  - Support children's learning at home
  - Communicate with teachers easily
  - Ensure fees are paid on time
  - Know about school events and holidays
  - Identify if children need extra help

### Pain Points
- Limited visibility into daily school activities
- Only learns about problems during quarterly meetings
- Doesn't know how to help with homework
- Misses school announcements
- Difficult to track fee payments and dues
- Can't communicate with teachers easily

### Behaviors & Preferences
- Checks phone during commute and breaks
- Prefers push notifications for important updates
- Wants weekly/monthly summaries, not daily details
- Values clear, concise communication
- Needs mobile-first experience

### User Stories
1. "As a parent, I want to receive weekly progress reports so I know how my child is performing without waiting for meetings"
2. "As a parent, I need to see attendance records so I can ensure my child is attending all classes"
3. "As a parent, I want to pay fees online so I don't have to visit the school or send cash"
4. "As a parent, I need to receive important school announcements so I don't miss critical information"
5. "As a parent, I want to message teachers directly so I can discuss my child's progress or concerns"

### Success Criteria
- Receives weekly progress updates
- 100% awareness of school events
- Pays fees on time without reminders
- Can identify learning issues early

- **Role**: Platform Administrator

- **Location**: Remote
- **Tech Savviness**: Very High
