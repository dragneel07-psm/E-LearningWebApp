## A.0 Submission Format Rules
1. **Two-envelope system:** Submit a sealed **Technical Proposal** and a separately sealed **Financial Proposal (Annex D)**; place both inside one outer sealed envelope, or submit via the **e-GP system** **[ADAPT]**.
2. Label each envelope with the RFP reference, bidder name, and "**TECHNICAL**" / "**FINANCIAL**" — **do not** include any price information in the Technical Proposal.
3. All forms must be on company letterhead, signed by the authorized signatory, and stamped with the company seal.
4. Page limit for the narrative (excluding forms/annexes): **[60] pages**, A4, font ≥ 11pt. Number every page.
5. Submit **one original + [two] copies** (hard copy) plus a **searchable PDF on USB** **[ADAPT]**; in case of discrepancy, the **signed original governs**.
6. Late, unsigned, conditional, or improperly sealed bids may be rejected.
