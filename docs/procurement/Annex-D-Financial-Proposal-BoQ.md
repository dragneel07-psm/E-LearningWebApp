2. Fill **every** line item. Items left blank are deemed quoted at **zero** and must still be delivered at no cost.
3. Prices must be **fixed and firm** for the proposal validity period and, for recurring items, for the full contract term unless an indexation basis is stated and approved.
4. The Financial Proposal must be submitted in a **separate sealed envelope** (or financial section of the e-GP system) from the Technical Proposal.
5. Itemize per **Lot** (Lot 1 Core, Lot 2 ERP, Lot 3 AI/Analytics – optional). Evaluation uses the **5-Year TCO (D.5)**.
6. In case of discrepancy between unit price and total, the **unit price governs**; between figures and words, **words govern**.
