# Part 1 — Subscription Agreement

**Between** [Local Government] ("the Client") **and** [Vendor] ("the Provider").

### 1. Documents & Precedence
The Agreement comprises, in order: this Agreement; the SLA (Part 2); the Data Processing terms (Part 3); the RFP and addenda; the Provider's accepted Technical Proposal (Annex F-2/F-3); and the Financial Proposal (Annex F-4). Higher-precedence documents prevail in conflict.

### 2. Service & Term
2.1 The Provider grants the Client and its in-scope community schools a subscription to the hosted ELMS, including provisioning, onboarding, training, support, updates, and availability per the SLA.
2.2 Term: **[2–3] years** from the Effective Date, renewable by mutual written agreement.
2.3 The Client may add schools mid-term at the Annex F-4 per-additional-school rate.

### 3. Fees & Payment
3.1 Subscription fees per Annex F-4, payable **[annually in advance / quarterly]** against a valid tax invoice and a satisfactory monthly SLA report **[ADAPT]**.
3.2 No price increase during the term except an approved, capped indexation if stated in Annex F-4.

### 4. Provider Obligations
Deliver and maintain the service per the SLA; protect data per Part 3 and RFP §5; provide training, documentation, and helpdesk; apply security patches and upgrades at no extra cost; not degrade or discontinue features without equivalent replacement.

### 5. Client Obligations
Provide school lists/volumetrics (Annex F-1) and a focal point; facilitate onboarding and training; use the service lawfully; pay valid invoices.

### 6. Data Ownership & IP
The **Client/schools own all data**. The Provider retains ownership of the platform and grants a subscription license for the term. No Client data is used beyond operating the service.

### 7. Availability, Service Credits & Default
Service credits apply for SLA breaches (Part 2). Persistent S1 breaches or a serious unremedied data breach constitute material default permitting termination.

### 8. Confidentiality & Liability
Mutual confidentiality (survives termination). The Provider indemnifies the Client for data breaches attributable to the Provider, IP infringement, and negligence/wilful misconduct; **no liability cap** for data breach, confidentiality breach, IP infringement, or wilful misconduct.

### 9. Termination & Exit
9.1 For cause on uncured material breach (**[30] days**), or immediately for insolvency/serious breach; for convenience by the Client on **[60] days** notice paying for delivered service.
9.2 On exit: full data export in open formats (CSV/JSON), transition assistance (**[60–90] days**), and a certified secure deletion. No proprietary lock-in.

### 10. Governing Law & Disputes
Good-faith negotiation, then **arbitration/adjudication under [applicable Act] at [seat], in Nepali/English** **[ADAPT]**; governed by the laws of **[Nepal]**.

### 11. General
Force majeure; no assignment without consent; written notices; amendments only in writing; severability; entire agreement.

# Part 2 — Service Level Agreement (SLA)

### 2.1 Availability
Production uptime **≥ 99.5%** per calendar month, excluding agreed maintenance (scheduled off-peak with **[48h]** notice).

### 2.2 Severity, Response & Resolution
| Severity | Definition | Response | Resolution |
|---|---|---|---|
| S1 — Critical | Platform/login down, data breach, data loss | ≤ 1 hour | ≤ 4 hours |
| S2 — Major | Core function broken, no workaround | ≤ 4 hours | ≤ 1 business day |
| S3 — Minor | Function impaired, workaround exists | ≤ 1 business day | ≤ 5 business days |
| S4 — Low | Cosmetic / query | ≤ 2 business days | Next release |

### 2.3 Support & Reporting
Helpdesk (phone/email/portal) in **Nepali/English** during **[hours]**; emergency security patches within **72 hours**; monthly uptime & ticket report to the Education Unit.

### 2.4 Service Credits
| Monthly Uptime | Credit (% of monthly fee) |
|---|---|
| ≥ 99.5% | 0% |
| 99.0–99.49% | 5% |
| 98.0–98.99% | 10% |
| < 98.0% | 20% |

**Exclusions:** Client-caused issues, force majeure, agreed maintenance, third-party network/DNS outside Provider control (Provider must still notify and assist).

# Part 3 — Data Processing Terms (summary; full undertaking in Annex F-3)
- **Client = Controller; Provider = Processor**, processing only on Client instructions to operate the service.
- Security per RFP §5; **children's-data safeguards** (minimization, no profiling/marketing, PII redaction before third-party/AI processing).
- **Data residency** in Nepal / Government Cloud **[ADAPT]**; no sub-processor changes without prior written approval; flow-down obligations.
- Breach notification within **[24–72]h**; audit rights for the Client.
- Return/secure deletion on termination with certificate.
