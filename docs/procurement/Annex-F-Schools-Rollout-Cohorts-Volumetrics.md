## F.5 Capacity & Sizing Notes for Bidders
- Size for **peak** load (result publication, exam windows, term start), not average.
- Account for **multi-tenant noisy-neighbor** isolation across [N] schools.
- State assumptions (avg users/school, content volume/school, storage/student) used for sizing in the Technical Proposal.
- Connectivity profile (F.2 last column) informs the low-connectivity approach (RFP §4.3).
